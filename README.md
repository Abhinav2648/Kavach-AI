# 🛡️ KAVACH AI

AI-powered cyber fraud detection platform built for the Economic Times AI Hackathon 2026.

## Problem Statement
PS 6: AI for Digital Public Safety – Defeating Counterfeiting, Fraud & Digital Arrest Scams

## 🚀 What KAVACH AI Does

KAVACH AI helps users identify potential cyber fraud before they become victims.

Users can analyze:
- 📱 Suspicious SMS messages
- 🔗 Suspicious URLs
- 📸 Screenshots of messages/scams
- 🚨 Digital arrest and phishing attempts

The system provides:
- Threat/Safe classification
- AI confidence score
- Risk level
- AI-generated verdict
- Safety recommendations
- Scan history
- Cyber fraud emergency assistance

## 🤖 AI Integration

KAVACH AI uses Google Gemini for intelligent scam and fraud analysis, including multimodal screenshot analysis.
